Our Story

BUILT ON
INTEGRITY

Founded in 2014 in Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape, Vinceafrica Logistics is a proudly Black-owned South African company. We are B-BBEE Level 1 certified, built on values that never waver: honesty, punctuality, and genuine care for every client.

Our mission is to eliminate your logistical problems. From the first call to the final sign-off, every decision we make points to one thing — your complete satisfaction.

Our vision is bold: top 50 logistics companies in the world by 2050, serving Africa and the globe with integrity at our core.
