Who We Are

BUILT ON
INTEGRITY

Vinceafrica Logistics was founded in 2014 in Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape. We are a proudly Black-owned South African company, B-BBEE Level 1 certified, built on values of honesty, punctuality, and genuine care for every client.

Our mission is simple: eliminate your logistical problems. From the first phone call to the final sign-off, every person and every decision in our company is committed to your success.

Our vision is bold: to be among the top 50 logistics companies in the world by 2050, serving clients across Africa and the globe with integrity at our core.
